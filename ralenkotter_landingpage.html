Pocket Parking

Pocket Parking is an application that makes the process of finding an open parking spot easy and convenient. Just search for the specific lot you would like to park in, and let Pocket Parking do the rest.

Field Research

Multiple interviews were conducted with NKU students to discuss the current parking situation. Several students said that they have trouble finding parking before class regularly, and some even said that there has been cases where being unable to find parking held them from making it to class at all.
